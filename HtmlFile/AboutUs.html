About Us

Welcome to The Resort!

At The Resort, we believe that great food and genuine hospitality are the cornerstones of a memorable dining experience. Located in the vibrant heart of "02 DOHS,Mirpur, Dhaka" our restaurant is dedicated to creating a space where flavor, comfort, and community come together.

Our Story

The Resort was born from a passion for culinary excellence and a love for bringing people together. Since opening our doors in 2022, we’ve strived to craft a dining experience that reflects our commitment to quality and creativity. Our menu offers a delightful array of dishes that celebrate both familiar comforts and bold new flavors.Our talented chefs, led by Mr. Jamal Khan or “our dedicated culinary team”, work tirelessly to source the freshest local ingredients and transform them into beautifully crafted dishes. From our beloved Lobster With Meat to our indulgent Sour Cherry, apple juice with Aronia, every bite is designed to delight and satisfy.

Our Philosophy

At The Resort, we’re more than just a restaurant; we’re a gathering place where memories are made. Our philosophy is simple: serve exceptional food in a welcoming environment where every guest feels like family.

Join Us

Whether you’re here for a special celebration, a casual meal, or a quick bite, Nectar & Nosh invites you to relax, enjoy, and savor every moment. Explore our menu, meet our team, and discover why we’re passionate about what we do.


Thank you for making The Resort a part of your dining journey. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
