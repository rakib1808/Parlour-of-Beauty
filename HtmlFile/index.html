MOST SAFE & RATED PARLOUR.

Welcome to The Wave Resorts Ltd, the most safe and top-rated beach-view resort in cox’s bazar. Experience quality rooms, unparalleled hotel services, and a gourmet food lounge. Our modern conference hall is perfect for hosting meetings and events. Experience oceanfront dining at its finest with the most authentic and fresh seafood and BBQ dishes at our sea-view restaurant. With stunning views of the sea, we offer a relaxing and luxurious stay for all guests. At The Wave Resorts Ltd, your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities. We take pride in offering attentive service, modern amenities, and a commitment to sustainability. Whether you’re seeking a romantic escape, a family adventure, or a corporate retreat, we’re here to ensure your stay is truly extraordinary

Questions We Get
Asked The Most

How close is the resort to the beach?

The resort is conveniently located just 25-30 meters away from the beach, offering easy access to the beautiful shoreline for our guests to enjoy.

Is the beach swimmable, and are there lifeguards on duty?

The beach offers refreshing swims, and lifeguards are on duty during peak hours for your safety.

Is there Wi-Fi available at The Wave Resorts Ltd?

Yes, The Wave Resorts Ltd. offers high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the resort to keep you connected.

Does The Wave Resorts Ltd. have parking available?

Yes, The Wave Resorts Ltd offers convenient and secure on-site parking for guests.

What is the check-in and check-out time at The Wave Resort Ltd.?

The standard check-in time is at 2 pm and check-out time is at 12 pm. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.
